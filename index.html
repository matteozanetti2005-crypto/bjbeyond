Manifesto

The question
no algorithm
can answer.

We live in an age of unprecedented machine intelligence. Algorithms generate images, compose music, write prose. And yet something persists — ineffable, stubbornly human.

I work in the space between. Analysing art markets with the rigour of financial data. Telling stories that machines can approximate but never originate. Questioning what remains when intelligence is no longer scarce.

The answer, I believe, lives in the soul.

"The work of art is the exaggeration of an idea."
— André Gide

"Intelligence without soul is merely computation.
Art without intelligence is merely decoration."
— BJ Beyond

I operate at the intersection of art market intelligence, digital publishing, and the philosophy of human creativity in an age of machine learning.

My work is data-driven but never cold. I believe that numbers, when read correctly, tell deeply human stories — about desire, status, beauty, and the desperate need to leave a mark.

I publish art market analysis under the handle @bj_artint on X, produce Italian-language content for collectors, and write long-form narratives about the artists and moments that shaped contemporary value.

I collect. I write. I question. I track the soul of the market — and occasionally, the market of the soul.
